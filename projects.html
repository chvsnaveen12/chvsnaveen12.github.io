74HC logic computer

Built a computer, a graphics card, a keyboard buffer out of pure 74HC logic. Wrote my own ISA, Micro-code, Assembler, OS and some games. Currently working on adding WI-FI and Sound capabilities. View more.

The amount of work and money which has gone into this is INSANE.

  • 2 years of constant learning from MITOCW and Ben-Eater
  • 1 month of planning and ordering parts.
  • 6 months of continous building
  • 2 month of some of the most frustrating debugging
  • 1 month to code the assembler and the Operating-System

Where does this leave me? This journey unknowingly made me complete 2 semesters worth of of B-Tech Computer Science. I am learning Verilog and Bluespec to polish my skills further. I am planning to intern at project SHAKTI of IIT-M after my college admissions. As for now highschool's got my focus :)
